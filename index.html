About: HONDA-CBR 300R

Honda CBR300R is expected to launch in India in December 2021 in the expected price range of ₹ 2,00,000 to ₹ 2,30,000. Currently available bikes which are similar to CBR300R are KTM RC 200, Suzuki Gixxer SF 250 & Bajaj Dominar 400. Another bike similar to CBR300R is CFMoto 300SR which is launching in October 2022 in India.

The Honda CBR300R is a bigger-displacement version of the CBR250R. When Kawasaki debuted the Ninja 300 as a response to the CBR250R, everyone and their uncle upped their displacement game. Along came Yamaha with 320cc and two cylinders, KTM with 373cc and one cylinder, and, recently, Kawasaki, with 400cc and two cylinders. The CBR300R takes what the CBR250R offers and turns it up a notch. 286cc from a single-cylinder liquid-cooled motor generates 30bhp and 27Nm, a healthy increase from the CBR250R's figures.
